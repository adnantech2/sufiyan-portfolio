Hi, I'm Sufiyan Ahmad.

I am a Postdoctoral researcher in the Bhamla Lab at Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, USA.

My current research interests are Organismal Biophysics and Soft entangled systems.

For my PhD, I have worked on the ensemble dynamics in tabletop active matter systems. I also have experience in a

variety of nonlinear experimental systems studying

phenomena like synchronization, oscillation quenching, and stochastic resonance.
